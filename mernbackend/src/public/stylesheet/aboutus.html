About Us

What is book harbour?

Book Harbour is a crowdsourced platform for book reading. It is a network of reading enthusiasts where the members read books by lending to and borrowing from each other.

Why Choose Us?

Book Harbour is a start-up co-founded by a small team of book enthusiasts. Being in love with books, and being passionate about reading are the only things that matter to us.

At "THE BOOK HARBOUR," we're rewriting the story of education. We know that when you close the chapter on your studies, your books often gather dust, their pages filled with knowledge waiting to be shared.

We believe in the power of books and the valuable notes tucked inside. That's why we created "THE BOOK HARBOUR," a haven for those looking to give new life to their educational companions.

Our Mission

To connect students and educators, fostering a community of learners.

To make education more affordable and eco-conscious.

To reduce the environmental footprint by giving textbooks a second voyage.
